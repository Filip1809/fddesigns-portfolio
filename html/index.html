Featured Projects

Lind Old Boys Community & Fitness Hub

This project involved redesigning the website for Lind Old Boys using Bootstrap, HTML, and CSS. The goal was to create a simple, clean, and user-friendly design that showcases the club’s commitment to community and fitness. The new layout focuses on easy navigation, allowing members to stay updated on upcoming events, membership prices, and contact management. Despite being a straightforward design, it highlights the importance of simplicity and clear information for an intuitive user experience.
